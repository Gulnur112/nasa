Return to Earth

NASA and its international partners will soon receive scientific research samples and hardware from the International Space Station. The SpaceX Dragon spacecraft with the scientific research and hardware will splash down at approximately 1:45 a.m. EDT on Sunday, May 25, off the coast of California. NASA will post updates on the agency’s space station blog but will not stream the splashdown.
